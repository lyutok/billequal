Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I split a bill?

A: Enter the total amount, add each person's share, and click "Calculate." The app will show each person's total instantly.

Q: Can I share results with friends?

A: Yes! Tap the "Share Results" button to send everyone's totals via your preferred app.
